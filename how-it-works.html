How It Works

Every RITTX LLC engagement follows the same five steps. You see the scope, the USD price and the delivery date before anything is charged.

The Engagement Process

Scope, Approve, Pay, Receive, Review

We do not take payment before a written Service Order is agreed, and we never ask for card details by phone, email, text or chat.

1

Request a Written Scope

Use the contact form or call +1 (832) 569-7145. Tell us the service you need, the deliverables you expect, the systems we will need access to and your target date. There is no cost or obligation at this stage.

We will confirm whether the work fits within the services we actually deliver. If a request falls outside our scope — for example lead sourcing, cold calling, escrow, brokerage or anything requiring a licence we do not hold — we will say so and decline it rather than take the work.

2

Review the Service Order

We send a written Service Order that states the exact service and deliverables, the total price in U.S. Dollars, the billing frequency, the start date, the delivery schedule, the number of included revisions, what we need from you, and direct links to our Terms of Service, Refund & Cancellation Policy and Service Delivery Policy.

Nothing is charged while you review it. Ask for changes and we will reissue the Service Order.

3

Approve and Pay Securely

You accept the Service Order in writing or electronically, then pay through the secure hosted invoice or checkout page linked from it. Before payment you confirm — with a required checkbox — that you have read our Terms of Service, Refund & Cancellation Policy and Service Delivery Policy.

The checkout page shows the service name, the scope summary, the amount and currency, the delivery date, any recurring terms and renewal date, our support phone and email, and the descriptor that will appear on your statement: RITTX SUPPORT. An itemized receipt is emailed automatically once payment succeeds.

RITTX LLC does not collect or store full card numbers, and does not accept card details over the phone, by email, text or chat.

4

We Perform and Deliver

Work begins once the Service Order is accepted, payment requirements are met and you have provided the access, content and instructions we need. RITTX LLC staff and contractors perform the work directly — we do not resell another company's service.

Deliverables are provided electronically by email, secure file link, an approved project-management system, or directly inside an account you have authorized. You receive status updates during the engagement, and we document any date change caused by missing access, content or approvals.

5

Review, Revise and Support

You have 5 business days to review the deliverable and tell us about anything that does not match the Service Order. Included revisions must be requested during that period and must stay within the original scope. If we hear nothing within the review period, the deliverable is treated as accepted — subject to any rights that cannot legally be waived.

Support questions go to support@rittx.com or +1 (832) 569-7145 during published business hours. Billing questions should come to us first so we can investigate before you raise a dispute with your card issuer; this does not limit your lawful dispute rights.

Payment Restrictions

RITTX LLC accepts online payments only for administrative and digital services directly provided by RITTX LLC under a written Service Order. The online payment system may not be used for property purchase prices, earnest money, option fees, assignment fees, escrow, title or closing funds, security deposits, investment contributions, mortgage or loan payments, brokerage commissions, contractor pass-through payments, lead purchases, telemarketing services or any payment owed to a third party.

RITTX LLC may cancel and refund any payment that does not match an approved Service Order. See the full Payment Restrictions page.
