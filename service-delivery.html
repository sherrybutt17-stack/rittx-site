Service Delivery Policy

How and when RITTX LLC delivers, what we need from you, and how acceptance works.

Last Updated: July 23, 2026  |  Company: RITTX LLC  |  Applies to: all services purchased through https://rittx.com/
Related policies: Terms of Service · Refund & Cancellation · Payment Restrictions

Nature of the Services

RITTX LLC provides administrative and digital support services. All services are performed remotely and all deliverables are provided electronically. Nothing is physically shipped, so no shipping charges, delivery addresses or carrier tracking apply to any purchase made on this website.

Delivery Method

Services and deliverables are provided by one or more of the following, as stated in your Service Order:

  • email to the contact named on the Service Order;
  • a secure file link;
  • an approved project-management or file-sharing system; or
  • work performed directly inside an account you own and have authorized us to access.

Start Date

Work begins once all three of the following are true: (1) you have accepted the Service Order, (2) the payment requirements stated in it are satisfied, and (3) you have provided the access, content and instructions we need. We confirm the start date in writing.

Delivery Timelines

The estimated timeline for your engagement is stated in your Service Order and controls if it differs from the table below. Unless otherwise stated, standard projects are delivered within 5–10 business days after all required materials are received. Monthly services are performed during the applicable paid service period.

Package Standard delivery window Billing period
Admin Support Sprint Up to 10 service hours used within 10 business days of access One-time
CRM Setup & Cleanup 7–10 business days after access and data are received One-time
Monthly Virtual Support Performed throughout the paid calendar month Monthly, charged at the start of each service period
Website Care Plan Each update request actioned within 3 business days Monthly, charged at the start of each service period
Marketing Design Pack Initial drafts within 5 business days of receiving all content One-time
Custom Written Scope As stated in the Service Order, typically 5–10 business days As stated in the Service Order

Business days are Monday to Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays. Business hours are 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM Eastern Time.

Client Dependencies

To deliver on schedule we need from you:

  • lawful access credentials, granted at the lowest permission level that allows the work, through your own administrative console;
  • the content, data and files the Service Order lists, in a usable format;
  • written instructions, brand or template requirements, and approvals when requested; and
  • a named point of contact who can answer questions and give approvals.

Client Delays

If access, content or approvals are not provided on time, the delivery date may be extended. RITTX will document in writing what is outstanding and the revised delivery date. Time reserved for your engagement that cannot be used because of an unresolved dependency may still be chargeable, as set out in the Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Progress Updates

For project work we provide a status update at least once per week until delivery. For monthly plans we provide a written activity summary at the end of each service period.

Acceptance

You should review deliverables within 5 business days of delivery and tell us about any material nonconformity with the Service Order. Included revisions must be requested during this review period and must remain within the original scope. If no issue is reported within the review period, the deliverable is considered accepted, subject to any rights that cannot legally be waived.

Revisions and Change Requests

The number of included revision rounds is stated in your Service Order — for example, the Marketing Design Pack includes two revision rounds per item. Work that changes the agreed scope requires a written change order and may incur additional fees, which will be quoted in USD before the work is done.

Handover and Access Removal

At the end of an engagement we provide any final files and configuration notes described in the Service Order, and we ask you to revoke the access you granted us. We return or delete client credentials and files when they are no longer needed, subject to our legal and accounting obligations. See our Privacy Policy for retention detail.

Support

Questions about delivery may be sent to support@rittx.com or raised by phone on +1 (832) 569-7145 during published business hours. We acknowledge support messages within one business day.

What Is Not Delivered Through This Website

No physical goods, no brokerage or licensed real-estate services, no escrow or fund-holding services, no leads or contact lists, and no service performed by an undisclosed third party. See Payment Restrictions for the full list of transactions that cannot be processed here.
