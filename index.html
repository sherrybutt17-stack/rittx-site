What We Sell

Defined Services, Delivered Digitally

Every service below is performed directly by RITTX LLC staff and contractors, invoiced by RITTX LLC, and supported by RITTX LLC. Each package has written deliverables, a fixed USD price and a stated delivery window.

🗂️

Virtual Administrative Assistance

Inbox organization, calendar scheduling, data entry, file naming, task follow-up and meeting notes. Billed as monthly hours or a fixed-scope project.

🧩

CRM Administration

CRM setup, authorized contact import, tagging, duplicate cleanup, pipeline configuration and one user-training session. One-time setup with optional monthly maintenance.

Website Maintenance

Workflow & Automation Setup

Building client-approved workflows using the client's own CRM, forms, calendars and productivity tools, with documented configuration notes.

Clear Boundaries

What RITTX LLC Does Not Do

We state our limits publicly so that clients, partners and payment providers know exactly what is and is not being sold on this website.

🚫

No Lead Sales

RITTX LLC does not sell leads, lead lists, exclusive leads, pay-per-lead packages, transferred calls or appointments as a product.

📵

No Cold Calling or Telemarketing

We do not perform unsolicited outbound calling, robocalling or outreach to purchased contact lists. Client support work is inbound and request-based only.

🏦

No Escrow or Transaction Funds

We do not collect or hold property purchase prices, earnest money, option fees, assignment fees, closing funds, deposits or investment contributions.

⚖️

No Brokerage, Legal or Mortgage Services

RITTX LLC does not provide brokerage, appraisal, legal, tax, mortgage, lending or investment services, and does not negotiate real-estate transactions.

👥

No Third-Party Payment Collection

We do not accept card payments on behalf of agents, brokers, title companies, contractors or any other third party. RITTX LLC is always the seller of record.

📈

No Guaranteed Results

We do not guarantee sales, leads, closings, revenue, search rankings or engagement. We commit to performing the deliverables in the written Service Order.

The Process

From Request to Delivery in Five Steps

Nothing is charged before a written scope is agreed. You always know the service, the price in USD, the delivery date and the refund terms before you pay.

1

Request a Written Scope

Tell us the service you need, the deliverables, the access required and your target date.

2

Review the Service Order

We send a written scope with the total price in USD, delivery schedule, revision terms and links to every policy.

3

Approve and Pay

You accept the Service Order and pay through our secure hosted invoice or checkout. Card details are never collected by phone, email or chat.

4

We Deliver

RITTX LLC performs the agreed work and sends digital deliverables with status updates.

5

Review and Support

You review within the stated acceptance period and request any revisions included in your Service Order.

Payment Restrictions

RITTX LLC accepts online payments only for administrative and digital services directly provided by RITTX LLC under a written Service Order. The online payment system may not be used for property purchase prices, earnest money, option fees, assignment fees, escrow, title or closing funds, security deposits, investment contributions, mortgage or loan payments, brokerage commissions, contractor pass-through payments, lead purchases, telemarketing services or any payment owed to a third party.

RITTX LLC may cancel and refund any payment that does not match an approved Service Order. Full details are on our Payment Restrictions page.
