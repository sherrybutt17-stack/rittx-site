Work Samples

Examples of work delivered by RITTX LLC, published with permission and with private information removed.

How we publish samples: every example on this page was produced by RITTX LLC staff and contractors under a written scope. Website design & build

Marketing agency website — GrowthGuild

growthguild.us

RITTX LLC designed and built a multi-page marketing website for GrowthGuild, a marketing-automation and virtual-assistant agency. The engagement covered page structure, layout, service and testimonial sections, responsive behaviour across desktop and mobile, and publication to the client's own hosting.

What RITTX delivered

  • Site structure and page map
  • Homepage and interior page layouts
  • Service and testimonial sections
  • Responsive behaviour across screen sizes
  • Publication to client-owned hosting

Client-supplied

  • Brand assets and logo
  • Service descriptions and copy
  • Testimonial content
  • Hosting and domain

Boundaries observed

  • No stock content used without licence
  • No performance or ranking outcome promised
  • No access retained after handover
CRM administration

CRM and reputation-automation configuration

RITTX LLC configured the reputation and review-management module of a client's CRM platform, including automated review-response behaviour, response timing and campaign setup. Work was performed inside the client's own CRM account under credentials the client granted and later revoked.

What RITTX delivered

  • Review-response automation configured
  • Response timing and wait rules set
  • Review request channels configured
  • Written handover notes

Client-supplied

  • CRM platform licence and account
  • Admin access, granted and later revoked
  • Approved response tone and templates

Boundaries observed

  • No contact data supplied or sourced by RITTX
  • No outbound calling or messaging performed
  • No review content fabricated or incentivised
  • Client data not used outside the engagement

Documentation Available on Request

For each engagement shown above, RITTX LLC retains the written scope, the invoice, the delivery correspondence and the client's permission to publish. Prospective clients, partners and payment providers may request this documentation by emailing support@rittx.com or calling +1 (832) 569-7145.

We do not publish client contact records, personal data, credentials or commercial terms, and we do not display a client's name or logo without written permission.
