Payment Restrictions & Acceptable Use

Exactly what our payment system may and may not be used for.

Last Updated: July 23, 2026  |  Company: RITTX LLC  |  Applies to: every payment accepted through https://rittx.com/
Related policies: Terms of Service · Refund & Cancellation · Service Delivery

Payment Restrictions Notice

RITTX LLC accepts online payments only for administrative and digital services directly provided by RITTX LLC under a written Service Order. The online payment system may not be used for property purchase prices, earnest money, option fees, assignment fees, escrow, title or closing funds, security deposits, investment contributions, mortgage or loan payments, brokerage commissions, contractor pass-through payments, lead purchases, telemarketing services or any payment owed to a third party. RITTX LLC may cancel and refund a payment that does not match an approved Service Order.

What We Accept Payment For

Card payments accepted on this website are always for a service that RITTX LLC performs itself, under a written Service Order that names the service, the scope, the amount in U.S. Dollars and the delivery date. RITTX LLC is the seller of record for every transaction, issues the invoice, provides support and carries the refund obligation.

The services we accept payment for are listed in full on our Services page and priced on our Pricing page: virtual administrative assistance, CRM administration, website maintenance, graphic design and property-marketing support, content formatting and publishing, inbound client support, and workflow and automation setup.

Transactions We Do Not Process

The following may never be paid through this website, by card or otherwise. If you need to move funds of these kinds, use a licensed title, escrow, lending or banking channel.

Not processed Includes
Real-estate transaction funds Property purchase prices, down payments, option fees, assignment fees, earnest money, security deposits and closing funds.
Escrow and fund holding Any arrangement where RITTX would hold or forward money for a buyer, seller, title company, agent or contractor.
Investment activity Investment contributions, syndications, crowdfunding, profit shares or any real-estate investment product.
Lending and debt services Mortgage payments, loan repayments, loan origination or servicing fees, foreclosure relief, debt settlement and credit repair.
Brokerage activity Sales commissions, referral fees, listing fees or any payment for licensed brokerage services.
Leads and telemarketing Lead lists, exclusive leads, pay-per-lead packages, transferred calls, appointments sold as a product, cold-calling and robocalling services.
Traffic and engagement Purchased website traffic, followers, likes, reviews or engagement of any kind.
Third-party payments Any amount owed to another company or individual, including contractor pass-through payments and payments for services RITTX does not perform.
Personal or test transactions Payments from cards belonging to RITTX owners, employees, contractors, friends or family made to create artificial processing history.

How We Enforce This

  • Every payment must correspond to an approved Service Order that names the service and scope.
  • Requests that fall within the restricted list are declined before a Service Order is issued.
  • If a restricted payment is received in error, RITTX cancels it and refunds it in full to the original payment method, normally within 5 business days.
  • RITTX will not split a transaction into smaller charges to work around a limit, and will not describe a service inaccurately on an invoice or in a statement descriptor.
  • Repeated attempts to route restricted funds through our payment system are grounds for terminating the client relationship.

How We Take Payment

  • Through a secure hosted invoice or checkout page linked from your Service Order.
  • In U.S. Dollars (USD), with the currency shown next to every amount.
  • With the service name, scope summary, amount, delivery date, recurring terms (where applicable), support contact and statement descriptor shown before you pay.
  • With a required checkbox confirming you have read the Terms of Service, Refund & Cancellation Policy and Service Delivery Policy.
  • Never by phone, email, text or chat — RITTX does not ask for card numbers, CVV codes or bank credentials through those channels, and does not store full card numbers.

Charges appear on your statement as RITTX SUPPORT. An itemized receipt is emailed automatically after payment.

Acceptable Use of Our Services

Clients may not use RITTX services for unlawful activity, deceptive marketing, spam, unsolicited telemarketing, unauthorized data scraping, document falsification, intellectual-property infringement, discrimination, harassment or prohibited financial activity. We may suspend or refuse work where a requested activity creates legal, privacy, payment-processor or card-network risk. This mirrors Section 14 of our Terms of Service.

Questions or Reports

If you are unsure whether a payment is permitted, ask us first. If you believe a payment was taken that should not have been, contact us immediately and we will investigate and refund where appropriate.
