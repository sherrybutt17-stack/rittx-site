Refund & Cancellation Policy

What you can cancel, what is refundable, how to request a refund and how long it takes.

Last Updated: July 23, 2026  |  Company: RITTX LLC  |  Applies to: all services purchased through https://rittx.com/
Related policies: Terms of Service · Service Delivery · Payment Restrictions

Purpose and Scope

This policy explains the cancellation and refund rules for administrative and digital services purchased directly from RITTX LLC under a written Service Order. It is incorporated into our Terms of Service, and you are asked to confirm you have read it before payment.

This policy does not apply to real-estate purchase agreements, escrow, deposits, title services or third-party services — none of which are sold or processed through this website. See Payment Restrictions.

Cancelling Before Work Begins

You may cancel at any time before work begins by emailing info@rittx.com with your name, company, invoice number and payment date. RITTX will refund the amount paid, less any clearly disclosed non-refundable third-party cost already incurred with your authorization (for example a stock-image or software licence purchased at your request).

Cancelling After Work Begins

Once work begins, you are responsible for completed work, time reserved for your engagement and approved third-party costs. Where RITTX approves a cancellation, we may issue a prorated refund for the undelivered portion of the Service Order.

Completed hours and accepted deliverables are non-refundable, except where a refund is required by law or where RITTX materially failed to provide the agreed service.

Completed Digital Deliverables

After a deliverable is accepted, or after the review period stated in your Service Order expires without a reported issue, fees for that deliverable are non-refundable except in the case of a verified material failure to match the written Service Order. Any revisions included in your Service Order remain available on the terms stated there.

Monthly Plans

You may cancel future renewals of a monthly plan at any time before the next billing date by emailing info@rittx.com. We confirm every cancellation in writing.

  • Cancellation prevents the next charge.
  • The current service period remains active through its end date and is not automatically refunded.
  • Unused hours or update requests do not roll over unless the plan expressly states otherwise.
  • There is no cancellation fee and no minimum term unless the Service Order states one.

If We Cancel

If RITTX cancels an engagement for a reason that is not your breach of the Terms — for example we determine we cannot deliver the agreed scope — we refund the fees paid for the undelivered portion in full.

RITTX may also cancel and refund in full any payment that does not correspond to an approved Service Order, including any payment that falls within our Payment Restrictions.

Duplicate or Incorrect Charges

Report a duplicate or incorrect charge to info@rittx.com within 30 days of the charge date. RITTX investigates promptly and corrects verified billing errors. Please contact us before filing a chargeback so we can resolve the matter directly; this does not limit your lawful dispute rights.

Refund Method and Timing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. RITTX does not refund by cash, cheque, store credit or to an unrelated account. We submit the refund within 5 business days after approval; your card issuer or bank may take additional time to post it to your account. Refunds are issued in U.S. Dollars (USD).

How to Request a Refund

Email info@rittx.com with:

  • customer and company name;
  • invoice number and payment date;
  • the service or package concerned; and
  • the reason for the request.

RITTX acknowledges every request within 2 business days and provides a written decision, with reasons, within 5 business days of receiving the information needed to assess it. You may also call +1 (832) 569-7145 during business hours to discuss a request.

Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy limits any right you have under applicable law that cannot be waived by agreement.
