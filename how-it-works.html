How It Works

Selling your house to RITTX LLC is simple, transparent, and stress-free. Here's what to expect.

Selling Your Home

Our Simple 3-Step Buying Process

When you work with RITTX LLC as a seller, you deal directly with us — no middlemen, no agent commissions, no guesswork.

1

Tell Us About Your Property

Fill out our simple contact form or give us a call at +1 (561) 726-3381. We'll ask about your property's location, size, condition, and your goals for the sale. This takes about 5–10 minutes and there's absolutely no obligation.

We work with all types of residential properties: single-family homes, condos, townhomes, duplexes, and more — in any condition, including homes that need major repairs or are facing foreclosure.

2

Receive a Fair, Written Cash Offer

Within 24 hours of our initial conversation, we'll present you with a written cash offer. Our offers are based on current market data, the property's condition, and comparable sales in your area — not lowball guesses.

We'll walk you through how we arrived at the number and answer any questions you have. There's no pressure and no deadline to accept. Take the time you need.

3

Close on Your Timeline

If you accept our offer, we move to closing — and you choose the date. We can close in as little as 7 days, or we can accommodate a longer timeline if you need time to move or make arrangements.

We handle all the paperwork, coordinate with the title company, and keep you informed at every step. On closing day, you receive your cash. No repairs required, no cleaning needed, no showings — just a clean, simple sale.

What We Buy

We Purchase All Types of Properties

RITTX LLC buys houses in a wide range of situations. If you need to sell, we're ready to make an offer.

🔨

Fixer-Uppers

Properties that need extensive repairs, renovations, or updates. We buy them as-is — you don't spend a dime fixing anything.

📉

Distressed Properties

Homes facing foreclosure, tax liens, or code violations. We work quickly to help homeowners out of difficult financial situations.

Relocation Sales

Moving for work, family, or a fresh start? We help you sell quickly so you can relocate without the stress of a lingering listing.

Inherited Properties

Landlord Exits

Ready to retire from being a landlord? We buy rental properties, including those with tenants in place.

Move-In Ready Homes

Your home doesn't have to be in poor condition for us to buy it. We purchase well-maintained homes too — any situation qualifies.
