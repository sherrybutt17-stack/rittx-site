Frequently Asked Questions

Billing, delivery, cancellation, revisions, data access and the work we do not take on.

Buying and Billing

What exactly am I paying for?

You are paying RITTX LLC for a defined administrative or digital support service, listed item by item in a written Service Order. The Service Order states the deliverables, the total price in U.S. Dollars, the delivery schedule, the number of included revisions and what we need from you. You are never charged before you approve it.

What currency are your prices in?

All prices, invoices and refunds are in U.S. Dollars (USD). The currency is shown next to every price on our Pricing page, on your Service Order, on the checkout page and on your receipt.

How do I pay, and do you take card details over the phone?

You pay through a secure hosted invoice or checkout page linked from your Service Order. RITTX LLC never asks for card numbers by phone, email, text or chat, does not key in cards manually on your behalf, and does not store full card numbers.

What will the charge look like on my statement?

Charges appear as RITTX SUPPORT. If you see a charge you do not recognize, email support@rittx.com or call +1 (832) 569-7145 and we will identify it for you.

Do I get a receipt?

Yes. An itemized receipt is emailed automatically after payment. It shows the seller of record (RITTX LLC), invoice number and date, the service and scope, the service period, the amount in USD, the expected delivery, the statement descriptor and our support contact details.

Do you offer services to individual consumers?

No. RITTX LLC sells business-to-business only, to real-estate agents, brokers, investors, property-management companies and similar businesses. The person accepting a Service Order must be at least 18 and authorized to bind the business.

Delivery and Revisions

When does work start and how long does it take?

Work begins once the Service Order is accepted, payment requirements are met and you have provided the required access, content and instructions. Standard projects are delivered within 5–10 business days of receiving everything we need; each package on the Pricing page shows its own window. Monthly services are performed during the paid service period.

How are deliverables sent to me?

Electronically — by email, secure file link, an approved project-management system, or as work performed directly inside an account you have authorized. Full detail is in our Service Delivery Policy.

What happens if I am slow to send access or content?

The delivery date may move. We will document in writing what is outstanding and the revised date. We do not start the clock until we can actually begin the work.

How long do I have to review the work?

5 business days from delivery. Tell us during that period about anything that does not match your Service Order and we will correct it. If we hear nothing within the review period, the deliverable is treated as accepted — subject to any rights that cannot legally be waived.

How many revisions are included?

It is stated in your Service Order — for example, the Marketing Design Pack includes two revision rounds per item. Revisions must be requested during the review period and stay within the original scope. New scope or changed instructions need a written change order and may cost more.

Cancellation and Refunds

Can I cancel before work begins?

Yes. Email support@rittx.com before work starts and we will refund what you paid, less any clearly disclosed non-refundable third-party cost we already incurred with your authorization.

What if I cancel after work has started?

You are responsible for completed work, reserved time and approved third-party costs. Where we approve a cancellation, we may issue a prorated refund for the undelivered portion. Completed hours and accepted deliverables are non-refundable except where required by law or where we materially failed to provide the agreed service. See the Refund & Cancellation Policy.

How do I cancel a monthly plan?

Email support@rittx.com before your next billing date. Cancellation stops the next charge. The current service period stays active through its end and is not automatically refunded. Unused hours or requests do not roll over unless your plan says otherwise.

How long do refunds take?

Approved refunds go back to the original payment method. We submit the refund within 5 business days of approval; your bank may take additional time to post it. We do not refund by cash, cheque or to an unrelated account.

I think I was charged twice. What should I do?

Report it to support@rittx.com within 30 days with the invoice number and payment date. We investigate promptly and correct verified billing errors. Please contact us before filing a chargeback so we can resolve it directly — this does not limit your lawful dispute rights.

Access, Data and Security

What access do you need to my systems?

The minimum required to perform the agreed work, granted through your own admin console at the lowest permission level that works. We ask you to revoke access when the engagement ends. We do not share credentials between clients and we do not ask you to email passwords.

Who owns the work you produce?

You keep the rights to everything you supply. On full payment you receive the rights to the final custom deliverables stated in your Service Order, excluding our pre-existing templates, tools, processes and any licensed third-party materials.

Do you sell or share my data?

No. RITTX LLC does not sell customer enquiries, contact lists or payment information. Where a subcontractor needs business-contact data to deliver your purchased service, they are bound by written confidentiality and data-handling obligations and we remain responsible. Details are in our Privacy Policy.

Will you contact my customers?

Only in response to enquiries they initiate, using templates you approve, and only if that is part of your Service Order. We do not make unsolicited outbound calls or messages, and we will not work from purchased or scraped contact lists.

Services We Do Not Provide

Can I pay you for real-estate leads or appointments?

No. RITTX LLC does not sell leads, lead lists, exclusive leads, pay-per-lead packages, transferred calls or appointments, and will not accept payment for them.

Can I send earnest money, a deposit or closing funds through your checkout?

No. Our payment system may not be used for property purchase prices, earnest money, option fees, assignment fees, escrow, title or closing funds, security deposits, investment contributions, mortgage or loan payments, or brokerage commissions. Use a licensed title, escrow or banking channel. Any such payment received here will be cancelled and refunded. See Payment Restrictions.

Can I pay you and have you forward it to my contractor or agent?

No. RITTX LLC is always the seller of record and only accepts payment for services it performs itself. We do not collect or pass on funds owed to a third party.

Can you list a property, negotiate, or advise on a transaction?

No. RITTX LLC is not a licensed brokerage and does not provide brokerage, appraisal, legal, tax, mortgage or investment services. Our work is administrative and produced under your direction and supervision.

Do you guarantee results?

No. We do not guarantee sales, leads, closings, revenue, search rankings, traffic or engagement. Our obligation is to perform the deliverables in your Service Order with commercially reasonable care.

Still Have a Question?

Call us during business hours or send a message and we will respond within one business day.
