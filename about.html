Who We Are

Real Estate, Done Right

RITTX LLC is a licensed Florida real estate company headquartered in Gainesville, FL. We specialize in the direct buying and selling of residential properties — meaning we are the principals in every transaction, not intermediaries or lead brokers.

Our business model is straightforward: we identify houses that need to change hands — whether homeowners facing difficult life situations, properties requiring repair, or sellers who simply want speed and certainty — and we purchase them directly at fair prices. We then renovate, improve, and resell those properties to buyers seeking quality homes in Florida.

We believe real estate transactions should be honest, transparent, and efficient. Our team handles every detail from offer to closing, so you never have to guess what comes next.
