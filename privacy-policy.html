Privacy Policy

What information RITTX LLC collects, why we collect it, who processes it and how long we keep it.

Last Updated: July 23, 2026  |  Company: RITTX LLC  |  Website: https://rittx.com/
Related policies: Terms of Service · Refund & Cancellation · Service Delivery · Payment Restrictions

1. Who We Are

RITTX LLC, 165 Seaman Ave 6L, New York, NY 10034, United States, operates https://rittx.com/ and provides business-to-business administrative and digital support services. This policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect information in connection with this website and those services.

Privacy questions: support@rittx.com or +1 (832) 569-7145.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact and business information you submit — name, business email, company name, phone number and the details of your enquiry.
  • Billing contact details — the name, business address and email used on your Service Order, invoice and receipt.
  • Service instructions and client-supplied files — the content, data and documents you send us so that we can perform the agreed work.
  • Account access data — the access you grant us to your own systems for the duration of an engagement. We ask for the lowest permission level that allows the work.
  • Communications — emails, messages, call notes and support correspondence.
  • Transaction metadata — invoice numbers, amounts in USD, payment status, dates, refunds, and the card brand and last four digits supplied to us by our payment processor.
  • Device and log data — IP address, browser type, pages viewed and referring page, collected when you visit this website.

We do not collect or store full payment-card numbers, CVV codes or bank credentials. Card details are entered directly on the hosted payment page operated by our payment provider and never pass through this website, or through RITTX email, text or chat.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information above to respond to enquiries, prepare written Service Orders, deliver the services purchased, process payments and refunds, provide support, secure accounts, detect and prevent fraud, keep accurate business and tax records, improve this website, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not use client-supplied data for any purpose other than delivering the engagement and meeting our legal and accounting obligations.

4. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by a third-party payment provider that maintains its own privacy and security practices and is responsible for handling card data under the applicable PCI DSS requirements. RITTX receives only transaction status and limited billing metadata — never the full card number.

The provider currently used for card payments is:

  • Stripe, Inc. — card-not-present payment processing, hosted checkout and hosted invoicing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

We name only providers actually in use. If we add or change a payment provider, we will update this section before the change takes effect.

5. Service Providers

We use a limited set of vendors to run the business — website hosting, business email, CRM, project management, cloud file storage and, where an engagement requires it, vetted contractors. These parties process information only for authorized business purposes, under written confidentiality and data-handling obligations, and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

6. We Do Not Sell Data or Leads

RITTX LLC does not sell customer enquiries, contact lists, lead data or payment information, and does not share personal information with third parties for their own marketing. Where business-contact data is shared with an authorized subcontractor in order to deliver a purchased service, RITTX remains responsible for appropriate contractual controls.

No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

7. Cookies and Analytics

This website uses only the cookies strictly necessary to serve the pages you request. It does not currently run advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels or third-party marketing tags. If we add web analytics or any other cookie-setting tool, we will name it here, state its purpose and retention period, and provide the opt-out method before it is enabled. We do not list tools that are not installed.

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings; this website will continue to function.

8. Marketing Email and Text Messages

We send marketing email or SMS only to people who have opted in. Consent to marketing messages is never a condition of purchasing any service, and transactional consent (scope confirmations, delivery updates, invoice notifications) is collected separately from marketing consent.

  • Email: every marketing message includes an unsubscribe link.
  • SMS: reply STOP to opt out of a program, or HELP for assistance. Up to 4 messages per month per program; message and data rates may apply; carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages; participants must be 18 or older.
  • You may also opt out at any time by emailing support@rittx.com.

Full SMS program terms are in Section 20 of our Terms of Service.

9. Data Retention

Record type Retention
Enquiries that do not become engagements 12 months from last contact, then deleted
Service Orders, invoices and receipts 7 years, for tax, accounting and dispute-evidence purposes
Support and delivery correspondence 3 years from the end of the engagement
Client-supplied files and working documents Returned or deleted within 30 days of the engagement ending, unless you ask us to keep them
Access credentials and permissions Revoked at the end of the engagement; we ask you to remove our access from your own console
Marketing and SMS consent records Kept while consent is active and for 4 years after opt-out, as proof of consent
Website server logs Up to 90 days

10. Security

We use access controls and least-privilege permissions, multi-factor authentication on business accounts where the platform supports it, a password manager rather than emailed or shared credentials, encryption in transit (HTTPS across this website), and a documented process for responding to suspected incidents. Contractors are engaged under written confidentiality and data-handling terms.

RITTX will never request card information, CVV codes or banking passwords by email, text or chat. If you receive such a request claiming to be from us, do not respond — contact us on the number above.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to have it corrected or deleted, to obtain a copy in a portable format, or to opt out of certain uses. To make a request, email support@rittx.com from the address on file, or call +1 (832) 569-7145. We will verify your identity before acting, respond within the period required by applicable law, and will not discriminate against you for exercising a right.

Some information cannot be deleted on request where we are required to retain it — for example invoices and payment records kept for tax and dispute-evidence purposes. Where an exception applies, we will tell you which one.

Where we hold information as a service provider on behalf of a client, requests from that client's own contacts should be directed to the client, and we will assist the client in responding.

12. Children

This website and these services are intended for businesses and are not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. International Users

RITTX LLC is based in the United States and information is processed there. If you contact us from outside the United States, you understand that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will post the revised version on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. Material changes affecting how we use information you have already given us will be notified by email where we hold a valid address for you.
