About RITTX LLC

A business-to-business administrative and digital support provider for real-estate professionals.

Who We Are

RITTX LLC is a United States limited liability company, registered in Florida and operating from its principal place of business at 165 Seaman Ave 6L, New York, NY 10034. We provide administrative and digital support services to other businesses — principally real-estate agents, brokers, investors and property-management companies that need reliable back-office help without hiring in-house staff.

Our work is deliberately narrow and clerical. We organize inboxes, calendars and files. We configure and clean up CRMs. We keep client-owned websites current. We produce marketing collateral from content our clients own. We build workflows inside the tools our clients already license. That is the whole of what we sell.

Every engagement is documented in a written Service Order before any work starts or any payment is taken. RITTX LLC is the contracting party, performs the work with its own staff and contractors, issues the invoice, handles support and carries the refund obligation. We do not act as an intermediary for another provider.

How We Work

Engagements are staffed by RITTX assistants, designers and technicians, some of whom are contractors engaged under written confidentiality and data-handling agreements. Every person working on an account is bound by the same rules: least-privilege access granted through the client's own admin console, no credential sharing, no use of client data outside the engagement, and prompt revocation of access when the engagement ends.

We keep separate contracts, invoice numbering and accounting records for this services business. Work is tracked so that each invoice can be tied to the scope agreed, the hours or deliverables produced, and the client's acceptance.

What We Are Not

Being explicit about our limits protects our clients, their customers and our payment providers. RITTX LLC is not:

  • a licensed real-estate brokerage, and we do not list, market as broker, negotiate or represent any party in a real-estate transaction;
  • a lead generator or lead seller — we do not sell lead lists, exclusive leads, pay-per-lead packages, transferred calls or appointments as a product;
  • a telemarketing company — we do not make unsolicited outbound calls, place robocalls or contact purchased lists;
  • an escrow agent, title company or fund holder — we never collect or hold purchase prices, earnest money, deposits or closing funds;
  • a lender, mortgage broker, debt-relief or credit-repair provider;
  • an investment company, and we do not accept investment contributions or offer any investment product;
  • a payment intermediary — we never accept card payments on behalf of an agent, contractor, title company or any other third party; and
  • a provider of legal, tax or appraisal advice.

If you need any of the above, we will tell you plainly that it is outside our scope and decline the work.

Claims We Do Not Make

We publish only statements we can support with records. We do not advertise transaction counts, client counts, revenue figures, average results or testimonials we cannot tie to an identifiable customer who has given permission. We do not guarantee sales, leads, closings, revenue, search rankings, traffic or engagement. What we commit to is performing the deliverables listed in your Service Order with commercially reasonable care, on the schedule stated, or telling you promptly if something will change.
