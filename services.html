Services

Exactly what RITTX LLC delivers, how long it takes and what we need from you.

Who these services are for: RITTX LLC sells to business customers only — real-estate agents, brokers, investors, property-management companies and related businesses. All work is performed by RITTX LLC staff and contractors under a written Service Order, invoiced by RITTX LLC and supported by RITTX LLC. We are the seller of record for every transaction.
Monthly hours or fixed project

Virtual Administrative Assistance

Day-to-day administrative support for a real-estate business, performed remotely by an assigned RITTX assistant during published business hours.

Deliverables

  • Inbox organization, labelling and triage
  • Calendar scheduling and appointment coordination
  • Data entry into client-owned systems
  • File naming and folder structure maintenance
  • Task follow-up and status tracking
  • Meeting notes and action-item summaries
  • Monthly written activity summary

Not Included

  • Cold calling or outbound telemarketing
  • Contacting purchased or scraped lists
  • Selling or transferring leads
  • Handling, holding or disbursing client funds
  • Signing documents on a client's behalf
  • Legal, tax, appraisal or brokerage advice

Delivery & Client Responsibilities

  • Monthly plans begin on the first business day of the paid service period
  • Project work delivered within 10 business days of receiving access
  • Client provides lawful account access and written instructions
  • Client approves any change of scope in writing
One-time setup + optional monthly maintenance

CRM Administration

Configuration and cleanup of a CRM that the client owns and licenses, using only contact data the client owns or is authorized to use.

Deliverables

  • CRM configuration to an agreed structure
  • Import of client-supplied, client-authorized contacts
  • Tagging and segmentation rules
  • Duplicate detection and cleanup
  • Pipeline and stage configuration
  • One live user-training session (up to 60 minutes)
  • Written configuration notes

Not Included

  • Supplying, sourcing or purchasing contact data
  • Data scraping or resale of personal data
  • CRM software licences or subscription fees
  • Sending marketing messages on the client's behalf without documented consent records
  • Guaranteed conversion, pipeline or revenue outcomes

Delivery & Client Responsibilities

  • Delivered within 7–10 business days after access and data are received
  • Client provides CRM admin access and the data file
  • Client confirms it owns or is authorized to use all imported data
  • Client attends the scheduled training session
Fixed project or monthly plan

Website Maintenance

Routine upkeep of a website the client owns, on hosting and platform accounts the client controls.

Deliverables

  • Text and image updates to existing pages
  • Landing-page edits within the existing design
  • Contact and enquiry form testing
  • Analytics installation and event checks
  • Broken-link and page-load checks
  • Plugin/theme update runs where the platform allows
  • Monthly maintenance report

Not Included

  • Hosting, domain or third-party subscription fees
  • Full redesigns or new site builds (quoted separately)
  • Remote device repair or unrelated technical support
  • Work on websites or accounts the client does not own
  • Guaranteed search rankings, traffic or engagement

Delivery & Client Responsibilities

  • Monthly plans run for the paid calendar month
  • Individual update requests actioned within 3 business days
  • Client provides CMS/hosting access and licensed content
  • Client owns all rights to supplied text and images
Per project or package

Graphic Design & Property Marketing Support

Design production from photos, logos and copy that the client owns or has licensed.

Deliverables

  • Property flyers and brochures (print-ready PDF)
  • Social graphics sized to the client's platforms
  • Listing-presentation layouts
  • Basic photo enhancement (crop, exposure, straightening)
  • Source and export files on completion

Not Included

  • Stock photography or font licences (client-purchased)
  • Photography or videography services
  • Use of images the client does not own or license
  • Misrepresenting a property's condition, price or status
  • Ad spend, media buying or campaign management

Delivery & Client Responsibilities

  • Initial drafts within 5 business days of receiving all content
  • Two revision rounds included per item
  • Client supplies photos, logo, copy and brand colours
  • Client confirms it owns or licenses all supplied content
Per project or monthly plan

Content Formatting & Publishing

Formatting and scheduling content that the client writes or approves, published to the client's own channels.

Deliverables

  • Formatting of client-supplied property descriptions
  • Newsletter and email-template layout
  • Social post formatting and scheduling
  • Proofreading for spelling, grammar and consistency
  • Publishing calendar in the client's tool

Not Included

  • Guaranteed traffic, sales, rankings or engagement
  • Sending to purchased or non-consented lists
  • Writing claims RITTX cannot verify
  • Buying followers, traffic or engagement of any kind

Delivery & Client Responsibilities

  • Standard turnaround of 5 business days per batch
  • Monthly plans performed within the paid service period
  • Client supplies and approves all content before publication
  • Client maintains its own consent and unsubscribe records
Monthly hours

Inbound Client Support

Response and routing support for enquiries that the client's existing customers initiate. Inbound only.

Deliverables

  • Responding to inbound emails and messages using approved templates
  • Scheduling appointments that customers request
  • Routing messages to the correct team member
  • Logging enquiries in the client's CRM
  • Monthly enquiry summary

Not Included

  • Unsolicited outbound calling or texting
  • Robocalls or automated dialling
  • Calls to purchased or rented contact lists
  • Appointment setting sold as a per-lead product
  • Taking card or bank details over the phone

Delivery & Client Responsibilities

  • Performed during published business hours in the paid month
  • Client supplies approved response templates and escalation rules
  • Client provides access to its own inbox and CRM
Fixed project

Workflow & Automation Setup

Configuration of client-approved workflows inside tools the client already licenses.

Deliverables

  • Workflow mapping document for client approval
  • Build-out in the client's CRM, forms and calendars
  • Testing of each configured step
  • Written handover notes and a walkthrough session

Not Included

  • Access to systems the client is not authorized to grant
  • Shared or reused credentials
  • High-risk remote technical support
  • Automations that send unsolicited outbound messages

Delivery & Client Responsibilities

  • Delivered within 7–10 business days of approval and access
  • Client grants least-privilege access via its own admin console
  • Client approves the workflow map before build
  • Client revokes access when the engagement ends

Payment Restrictions

RITTX LLC accepts online payments only for administrative and digital services directly provided by RITTX LLC under a written Service Order. The online payment system may not be used for property purchase prices, earnest money, option fees, assignment fees, escrow, title or closing funds, security deposits, investment contributions, mortgage or loan payments, brokerage commissions, contractor pass-through payments, lead purchases, telemarketing services or any payment owed to a third party.

RITTX LLC may cancel and refund any payment that does not match an approved Service Order. See the full Payment Restrictions page.
